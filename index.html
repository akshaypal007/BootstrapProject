With a photo we can capture a moment, and have it forever. I think we take that idea for granted, with photography being so ubiquitous these days.But seriously just take a second to appreciate that. If we did that in Ancient Greece they’d call us a sorcerer. Or a god.

"Photography changes the way we see things".It’s actually quite an incredible transformation to experience. Suddenly we notice light, shapes, colours, textures, people, bulidings, trees, flowers.

Once we start noticing details, we inevitably start to see how much beauty is all around us. Every day is filled with it—in the most ordinary or unexpected places.When we start to derive happiness from seeing some particularly awesome light.
